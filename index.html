Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines the approach of this App regarding the handling of private data, aiming to prevent the leakage of your data. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully.

1. Private Information
This application is a standalone app that does not collect any personal information from users, nor does it upload any user information.

2. Data Sharing Authorization
This application does not share any user data with third parties. All device configurations and settings remain exclusively on your device.

3. Information Storage
All request information collected by AVCmd will be stored on the current device. This information will not be uploaded to any server and will only be viewed and shared by the user.

4. Information Security
The HTTPS decryption certificate for each device is generated individually. Unless you actively share it, others cannot obtain your certificate.

5. Network Security
This application communicates with AV equipment over local network using TCP, UDP, Telnet and HTTP protocols. For HTTP connections requiring authentication, your login credentials are stored locally on your device. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your network and connected devices.

6. Privacy Concerns
If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy or data handling practices, please email us at alexastafew@gmail.com
